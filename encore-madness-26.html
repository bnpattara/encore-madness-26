🏀 Encore Madness '26

GAP INC. MID ATLANTIC · MARCH 8 – 31 · 3 WEEKS · POINTS ACCUMULATE ALL MONTH

📲 Zipline Verification — Required

Every card must be shouted out in the Zipline to earn bonus points. Each post must include:

  • Customer first name
  • Encore Gap card opened
  • Whether it was an Order In Store transaction
  • Number of units in the basket
Suggested format:
“🏀 Sarah — Encore Gap card, Order In Store, 4 units — SLAM DUNK 💥”
“⭐ Mike — Encore Gap card, 3 units — AND ONE”
“✅ Jordan — Encore Gap card”
⚠️ No post = no bonus. Cards without a Zipline shoutout earn base points only. No Slam Dunk or And-One bonus without the post. Associates post. Leaders make sure they do.

🏙️ Store Points

Action Points
Card opened 1 pt
Win your weekly rivalry matchup +5 pts added to cumulative total
×1.5 Floor Bonus Applied to all cards when 100% of associates participate that week
Example: 25 cards × 1.5 (100% floor) = 37.5 pts. Win the rivalry that week = 42.5 pts total.

⭐ Associate Points — District MVP Race

Action Points
Card opened 1 pt
And-One — 3+ item basket + card (Zipline required) 2 pts total
Slam Dunk — Order In Store + card (Zipline required) 3 pts total

Associate points are flat — no floor bonus applies. Points accumulate across all 3 weeks. Top associate = District MVP.

🏆 Bracket Structure

Championship Bracket — Top stores by cumulative points. Re-seeded each week.

NIT Redemption Track — Bottom stores. Any NIT store that crosses their strike rate goal by end of Week 3 wins a team prize.

Weekly Rivalries — Head-to-head store matchups per week. Week 1 seeded by opening rate. Winning your matchup = +5 pts.
