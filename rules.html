📋 Rules & Prizes
GAP INC. MID ATLANTIC · MARCH 8 – 31 · 3 WEEKS · POINTS ACCUMULATE ALL MONTH

📅 Competition Timeline

Week 1
March 8 – 14
Seeding week — Week 1 rates set rivalry matchups
Week 2
March 15 – 21
Rivalries in full effect — re-seeded from Week 1
Week 3
March 22 – 31
Final push — cumulative winner takes all
Points accumulate across all 3 weeks. Each week's data is added to the running total. The competition closes March 31.

🎯 The Goal

2.2% strike rate — 1 card opened per 45.4 transactions. This is the only threshold that matters. Everything else is built on top of it.

📲 Zipline Verification — Required

Every card must be shouted out in the Zipline to earn bonus points. Each post must include:

  • Customer first name
  • Encore Gap card opened
  • Whether it was an Order In Store transaction
  • Number of units in the basket
Suggested format:
“🏀 Sarah — Encore Gap card, Order In Store, 4 units — SLAM DUNK 💥”
“⭐ Mike — Encore Gap card, 3 units — AND ONE”
“✅ Jordan — Encore Gap card”
⚠️ No post = no bonus. Cards without a Zipline shoutout earn base points only. No Slam Dunk or And-One bonus without the post. Associates post. GMs make sure they do.

🏙️ Store Points

Action Points
Card opened 1 pt
Win your weekly rivalry matchup +5 pts added to cumulative total
×1.5 Floor Bonus Applied to all cards when 100% of associates participate that week
Example: 25 cards × 1.5 Floor Bonus = 37.5 pts. Win the rivalry that week = 42.5 pts total.

⭐ Associate Points — District MVP Race

Action Points
Card opened 1 pt
And-One — 3+ item basket + card (Zipline required) 2 pts total
Slam Dunk — Order In Store + card opened (Zipline required) 3 pts total
Hit 2.2%+ personal rate for the week +5 pt weekly bonus

Associate points are flat — the Floor Bonus does not apply. Individual performance is measured clean regardless of store. Points accumulate all 3 weeks. Top associate after Week 3 = District MVP.

🏆 Bracket Structure

Championship Bracket — Top 5 stores by cumulative points. Re-seeded each week by cumulative total.

NIT Redemption Track — Bottom 5 stores. Any NIT store that crosses 2.2% by end of Week 3 earns team lunch on Gap. Multiple stores can win.

Weekly Rivalries — 5 head-to-head matchups per week. Week 1 seeded by opening rate (1v10, 2v9, 3v8, 4v7, 5v6). Re-seeded by cumulative points for Weeks 2 and 3. Winning your matchup = +5 pts.

🏅 GM Leadership Floor

Status Criteria
Leadership Floor Active GM drives ≥90% associate participation
100% Floor 🥇 All associates participating → triggers ×1.5 store multiplier for that week

The Floor Bonus is recalculated weekly. A store must maintain 100% participation to keep the multiplier each week.
